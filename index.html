Our Story

Farming the way our grandparents taught us — with today's standards.

Blessed Agri began as a single family plot growing beans and maize for the local market. Over twelve years, that plot grew into a trusted supply chain reaching households, schools, and processors across the north.

We still plant, harvest, and grade every batch ourselves — no middlemen guessing at quality on our behalf.

  • Owned and operated by a local farming family
  • Crops graded and cleaned before sale
  • Supplying households, schools, and processors

Good to know

Do you sell in bulk or small quantities? +

Both. We supply single bags for households as well as bulk orders for mills, schools, and traders — call us to discuss volume pricing.

Which areas do you deliver to? +

We deliver across the Northern Region and can arrange transport for larger orders to other regions on request.

Is your produce organic? +

Yes — we follow sustainable, low-input farming practices across all our fields, without synthetic pesticides.

How do I place an order? +
