Questions

Good to know

Everything you need to know before placing your first order.

Do you sell in bulk or small quantities? +

Both. We supply single bags for households as well as bulk orders for mills, schools, and traders — call us to discuss volume pricing.

Which areas do you deliver to? +

We deliver across the Northern Region and can arrange transport for larger orders to other regions on request.

Is your produce organic? +

Yes — we follow sustainable, low-input farming practices across all our fields, without synthetic pesticides.

How do I place an order? +

Call or WhatsApp us on either of our numbers, or email us directly. We'll confirm availability and delivery.

Do you offer credit or payment terms for bulk buyers? +

For established wholesale partners we can discuss flexible terms — reach out to us directly to talk through your specific needs.

What packaging do you use? +

Crops are cleaned and bagged in standard market-weight sacks, with custom packaging available for larger institutional orders.

Can I visit the farm before ordering? +

Yes, we welcome visits from serious buyers — just call ahead so we can plan a time that works.
