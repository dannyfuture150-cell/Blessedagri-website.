Beans

Our beans are hand-sorted for size and quality, grown in the mineral-rich soils of the Northern Region. High in protein and fibre, they cook faster and hold their shape better than mass-market alternatives.

  • Available in bulk sacks or smaller retail bags
  • Cleaned and free of stones or husks
  • Harvested at peak dryness for longer shelf life

Maize

Full, golden kernels dried naturally under the northern sun before milling or bagging. A staple for households, schools, and processors across the region.

  • Low moisture content for safer long-term storage
  • Suitable for milling, feed, or direct sale
  • Available year-round with seasonal fresh stock

Soya Beans
